My Favorite Places Collection

This is my collection of photos of places that I have visited since I have been with my fiancée. They are very special to me because they are memories that will last forever. I have visited more places with her in the last eleven months than I have in many years prior.

Puerto Penasco

Vacation | 2025 | Puerto Penasco

This is one of my favorite places that I have visited! The view is amazing and during September, the weather is great! We stayed at a beautiful resort right on the beach and enjoyed every moment of our time there. The beach was not crowded during this time of the year so we were able to truly relax. The time seemed like it slowed down. We enjoyed walking along the beach, collecting shells, and watching the sunset every evening. I cant wait to go back and sip on some drinks at the swim-up bar!

The Lodge

Weekend Getaway | 2025 | The Lodge

The Lodge in Cloudcroft, New Mexico was an amazing place to stay. We like the rustic look of the Lodge and enjoyed some wine next to the fireplace at the entrance. We met some really nice people and later on that night we went for a ghost hunt to try and find Rebecca, the ghost that is said to haunt the Lodge.

The Oasis

Austin Trip | 2025 | The Oasis

The Oasis in Austin, Texas is a beautiful place located on the cliffs overlooking Lake Travis. My fiancée and I were able to make it at the perfect time to see the sunset. It was a special moment for both of us.

Cloudcroft

Getaway | 2025 | Cloudcroft

Cloudcroft is one of my favorite mountain towns to visit. Its about an hour and a half drive from El Paso. It is a place that my fiancée and I like to go to get some fresh air with about a twenty degree drop in temperature compared to El Paso. We enjoy walking around the village and exploring the shops. We hike on trails that are right down the road from the village. We always come across wildlife on our hikes such as the mule deer.

Lady Bird Lake

Austin Trip | 2025 | Lady Bird Lake

Lady Bird Lake was an amazing place to visit. We rented kayaks and went around the lake for a couple of hours. The views of the city from the lake are amazing! I highly recommend this place to anyone visiting Austin, Texas. Make sure to paddle in the right direction against the current when you start and with the current when you return the kayaks. We made that mistake and it was tiring!

Salt Lick BBQ

Salt Lick BBQ | 2025

My fiancée and I love BBQ and we heard about Salt Lick BBQ in Driftwood, Texas from one of her coworkers. Some of the best BBQ I have had! I recommend stopping by Fredricksburg and doing some wine tasting on your way to Salt Lick.
